Corpus Analysis

For my corpus analysis, I used Voyant Tools to explore word frequency patterns in the text. The Cirrus word cloud shows which words appear the most and gives insight into what characters and themes dominate the writing.

Larger words like carlos, almeyda, pocomo, and eugenia indicate the main characters. Smaller words like “sir,” “shall,” and “enter” show the dramatic, play-like structure of the text. This visualization helps reveal tone, character importance, and the emotional intensity of the story.
